About Our Resort

Nestled along the pristine coastline of Sabangan, Ilocos Sur, Sabangan Beach Resort offers a tranquil sanctuary away from the hustle and bustle of city life. We pride ourselves on providing an authentic Filipino hospitality experience, surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty.

Our resort is designed to blend seamlessly with the lush tropical environment, offering comfortable accommodations and a wide array of activities for all ages. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or a romantic getaway, Sabangan Beach Resort is your perfect destination.
